Our Story

THE ARTIST
BEHIND THE
LENS

Hey there! I'm the creative force behind TOYZ FOR SHOTZ, a Chicago-based toy photographer on a mission to elevate action figure photography to fine art status.

What started as a hobby sharing photos of my growing collection has evolved into a full-blown passion project. I specialize in creating dramatic, cinematic shots that make your favorite characters look like they just stepped off the big screen.

From Mortal Kombat fatalities to Star Wars epic battles, every shot tells a story. I use custom-printed backdrops, professional lighting setups, and careful posing to bring these plastic heroes to life.

Our Mission

To inspire collectors worldwide to see their toys differently, and to build a community of passionate photographers who share this unique art form.
